Who We Are

Om Prayukti (ॐ प्रयुक्ति) — meaning "Applied Technology" — is an IT services company committed to delivering smart, scalable, and sustainable technology solutions.

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and everyday business needs, we serve startups, SMEs, and enterprises across India and beyond.

Our team of engineers, designers, and consultants brings deep expertise and genuine care to every engagement. We don't just build software — we build partnerships.
